# Hiring Manager Research Checklist

## Required Sections
- Identity and role (how confident the match is)
- Career history (companies, role progression, tenure lengths)
- Leadership/management signals (public posts, interviews, team comments)
- Pace and expectations (evidence of intensity or balance)
 - Source diversity check (LinkedIn + at least one other source)

## Output Format
Use short bullets with citations. Include at least one quote if available.
