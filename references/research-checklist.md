# Company Research Checklist

## Required Sections
- Company snapshot (what they do, stage, size, funding if available)
- Leadership history (founders and key execs, prior companies, tenure lengths)
- Culture signals (values, management style cues, pace expectations)
- Org changes (recent restructures, layoffs, strategy shifts)
- Strategy alignment (how this role fits current strategy)
- Source diversity check (company site + independent coverage + leadership profile)
- Leadership implications (what bios imply for maturity, pace, and decision style)
 - Founder media/announcements (posts, videos, or public announcements per founder if available)

## Output Format
Use short bullets with citations. Include at least one quote per section when possible.
