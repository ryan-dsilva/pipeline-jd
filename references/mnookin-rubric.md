# Mnookin Role Fit Rubric

## Immediate Disqualifiers (Gate)
If any are clearly present or strongly implied, mark **HARD PASS** but continue scoring.
1) Stage: Seed, Series A, or "Early Stage" (unless explicitly well-funded/mature)
2) Location: Not fully remote **and** not hybrid in San Francisco (SF). Any non-SF on-site requirement is a HARD PASS.
3) Pay/Level: Clearly junior/mid OR max pay < $200k
4) Travel: Requirements for "extensive travel" or frequent on-site presence (>25% of time) outside of the primary hub. (Note: Light/occasional travel is acceptable).

## Section 1: Health & Maturity (Max 40)
- +20 Mature Org: Clear reporting lines, specialized roles, strategy focus
- +10 Sustainability: balance, wellness, thoughtful, long-term
- +10 Stable Leadership: Series C+ or public. Series B is in the gray zone.
- +10 Team Distribution: US-based with HQ in SF (10); US East Coast (7); Europe (5); widely spread timezones (â€‘10)
- -10 Manic Tone: grind, hustle, chaos, nights/weekends, whatever it takes

## Section 2: Role Fit (Max 30)
- +10 Core Product Focus: engagement, retention, core value
- +10 Applied AI Strategy: LLM/agent strategy (not just model building, not wrapper)
- +10 Domain Match: B2B SaaS, fintech, healthtech
- -10 Domain Mismatch: growth hacking, martech

## Section 3: Skill Match (Max 30)
- +10 Customer Research: direct customer interaction valued
- +10 Technical/Prototyping: technical background, prototyping
- +10 High Autonomy: roadmap, discovery, strategy leadership
- -10 Low Autonomy: command-and-control, top-down roadmap

## Persona and Intensity Signals
- Infer PM type: Craftsperson / Operator / Visionary
- Estimate expected hours per week (6 months in)
- Assess politics level based on org changes and strategy alignment

## Research Requirements
- Company background and leadership history
- Hiring manager (if known or inferred)
- Glassdoor work-life balance signal
- Product strategy, competitors, differentiators, key changes
